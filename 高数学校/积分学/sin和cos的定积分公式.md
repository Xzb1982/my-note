n ∈ N或 n=0时，那么 \int^{\frac{\pi}{2}}_{0} \cos^n xdx =\int^{\frac{\pi}{2}}_{0} \sin^n xdx \\
I_{0} = \int^{\frac{\pi}{2}}_{0} \cos^0 xdx = \frac{\pi}{2} \\
I_{1} = \int^{\frac{\pi}{2}}_{0} \cos^1 xdx = 1,I_{n} = \begin{cases}
\frac{n-1}{n} \frac{n-3}{n-2} \dots \frac{1}{2} I_{0},当n为偶数时 ,I_{0}就是 \frac{\pi}{2} \\
\frac{n-1}{n} \frac{n-3}{n-2} \dots \frac{2}{3} I_{1},当n为奇数时,I_{1}就是1
\end{cases}
