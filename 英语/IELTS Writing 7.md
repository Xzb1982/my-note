开头：背景句+任务回应
问题1（原因）原因1+解释+（例子）+原因2+解释+总结
问题2（解决方案）解决方案1+解释+（例子）+解决方案2+解释+总结
针对问题来写主体段，如果问题1是原因，问题2的解决方案，就这样写
结尾：总结全文观点+升华

模板
	**如果问Causes:原因1+解释+原因2+解释+例子+总结（两个原因的总结）**
		原因1:
			One of the primary driving forces behind (主题) is (原因1).
			This is largely due to the fact that (进一步解释原因1如何起作用), which consequently leads individuals to (具体行为/结果).
			For instance, a recent survey conducted by (机构名称) revealed that over 80% of surveyed individuals admitted that they are more inclined to (行为A) rather than (行为B) when faced with (相关情境).
		原因2：
			Another equally significant contributing factor is (原因2), which stems from (简短的背景解释).This often results in a tendency for people to favour (行为/选择A) over (行为/选择B), as (进一步解释因果关系).
		Concluding Sentence (二选一):
			总结两个原因（看得出主题）： These two factors, namely (原因1) and (原因2), collectively underscore the complexity of this issue.
			强调影响： This highlights the significant impact of these underlying causes on (相关领域/社会现象).
	**如果问Solution:方案1+解释+例子+方案2+解释+总结**
		Topic Sentence (过渡句，连接上一段):Regarding the solutions(多个方案) ......
		Given the multifaceted nature of these causes, addressing (主题) requires a combination of approaches at both（）and（） levels.
		Solution 1:
			To begin with, (方案1) can play a crucial role in mitigating the problem.
			For example, introducing (具体措施/课程/政策) in (相关场所，如学校/职场) can equip (受益群体) with the necessary tools and awareness to (预期效果).
			A successful initiative implemented at (机构/国家) has demonstrated that such interventions can (具体数据/结果).
		Solution 2 (递进):
			In addition to institutional efforts, (方案2) is equally imperative.
			Encouraging (具体行为，如定期休息/家庭沟通) can provide opportunities for (群体) to (积极行为) , which in turn promotes (更深层的积极结果).
		Concluding Sentence:
			Therefore, a synergy between (方案1) and (方案2) can contribute to a more (积极的结果，如 balanced / resilient / cohesive) society.
	**如果问Effect:影响1+解释+影响2+解释+例子--（总结放在conclusion）**
		Topic Sentence:
			Regarding the potential effects~~,~~ it is critical to recognise that (主题) exerts a considerable influence on both (影响方面1) and (影响方面2).
		Effect 1 + Explanation:
			On the one hand, the prevalence of (主题) has a profound impact on (影响方面1) , as evidenced by the fact that (具体解释机制) . This can manifest in (具体表现形式) .
		Effect 2 + Explanation:
			On the other hand, （这样的连接词是有好与不好的，因为这个连接词需要有对比关系）the consequences extend to (影响方面2) , with far-reaching implications stemming from (解释) . Individuals and communities alike are compelled to (应对方式) .
		Example (用数据或研究支撑):
			Recent official reports further elucidate this issue, revealing that (关键数据或研究发现) .
		Concluding Sentence:
			These findings collectively highlight the urgent need to acknowledge and address the far-reaching effects of (主题) .

我们可以从雅思评分标准里面得知，TR是唯一的分档标准，TR好，一般CC就好；这是因为考官要在3分钟里面改完一篇作文。而CC是TR的标志，而且如果TR的思路OK，CC也契合（例如句子之间的逻辑关系契合的话，那么TR不会低）

连贯与衔接 (CC) 6 段落结构，有连接词使用。但部分连接词使用不准确（如Namely），段落内部逻辑跳跃。

词汇资源 (LR) 5.5-6 有一定词汇量，但存在搭配错误和重复。（拉不了分）

语法范围与准确性 (GRA) 5.5-6（均线水平） 存在多处语法错误（主谓一致、介词、被动语态、词性），影响阅读流畅度。
