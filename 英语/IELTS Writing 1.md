The charts below show the number of reasons why some people leave Uk and why some people choose to stay.Summarise the information byselecting and reporting the main features, andmake comparisons where relevant.
![[Pasted image 20260514222751.png]]
The pie diagrams depict a number of factors ==**behind these individuals move**== away from the UK and the individuals prefer to remaining in the United Kingdom.
Overall,most of people choose to leave the UK because of Quality of Life. However,  Quality of Life is also the main reason why people stay in the UK.
Quality of Life and Financial Resources both reach a large proportion in the reason why people stay or leave the UK.For people who want to leave the UK, Quality of Life accounts for 34 percent and Financial Resources stands at 20 percent. For people who want to stay in the UK, Quality of Life falls at 40 percent and Financial Resources stands at 30 percent.
If considered separately, Weather, Employment and Join Family and Friends do not represent much proportion and these three would amount to 46 percent in the reason why people leave the UK, which is a considerable proportion （这里改成两个对比句）. But it is less than the total amount of Quality of Life and Financial Resources.Additionally ,These three would amount to 30 percent which is less than Quality of Life and equals to  Financial Resources in the reason why people stay in the UK.

1.behind these individuals move 语法不通--behind these individuals' decision to move
2.介词搭配错误： at 40 percent--accounts for / makes up / stands at，但 falls at 不标准。可以说 falls to 40%（下降到），但这里是静态描述，==**应用 stands at 或 is 40%**==。
3.less than the total amount of Quality of Life and Financial Resources比较对象不明确。是比“生活质量单独一项”小，还是比“生活质量+财务”之和还小
the combined proportion/share of A and B
4.which is a considerable proportion but less than the total amount … Additionally, These three would amount to 30 percent which is less than Quality of Life and equals to Financial Resources”试图在一次比较中说太多，反而削弱了对比。建议拆成两个清晰对比句。

对比可以更强烈更具备总结性--Overall， leavers are driven by a broad mix of frustrations, with secondary factors collectively surpassing finance; stayers, by contrast, are motivated overwhelmingly by Quality of Life itself, with finance matching all other minor reasons combined.
